Build the world's leading AI Front Desk platform for dental practices.

PatientPilot AI will help practices:

- Book appointments automatically
- Recover missed opportunities
- Reduce front desk workload
- Improve patient experience
- Increase practice revenue

Our long-term goal is to become the operating system for patient communication.

# Product Principles

We build only features that create customer value.

We prioritize quality over quantity.

Every sprint must produce production-ready software.

We optimize for customer success, not feature count.

---

# Decision Framework

Before building a feature, ask:

1. Does this help us close our first customer?
2. Will a dentist understand its value?
3. Does it improve the demo?
4. Does it reduce staff workload?
5. Does it increase trust?

If the answer is "No", postpone the feature.
