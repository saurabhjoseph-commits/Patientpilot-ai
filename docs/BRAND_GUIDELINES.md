# Brand Voice

Always write as if speaking to a busy dental practice owner.

Tone should be:

- Friendly
- Professional
- Confident
- Helpful
- Direct

Avoid:

- Buzzwords
- Excessive technical jargon
- Aggressive sales language

# Logo Usage

Always use the official PatientPilot AI logo.

Requirements

- Maintain clear space around the logo.
- Do not stretch or distort.
- Do not rotate.
- Do not change colors.
- Do not apply effects or shadows.

Preferred formats

- SVG
- PNG (transparent)

---

# Photography Style

Use:

- Modern dental practices
- Friendly staff
- Real patient interactions
- Clean reception areas
- Professional equipment
- Bright natural lighting

Avoid:

- Stock images that feel staged
- Dark environments
- Busy compositions
- Cartoon illustrations

---

# Iconography

Use Lucide icons in the application.

Marketing materials may use clean outline icons.

Icons should communicate clarity, not decoration.

---

# Illustration Style

Minimal.

Professional.

Healthcare-focused.

No cartoon characters.

No exaggerated AI robots.

---

# UI Style

The application should feel like:

- Stripe
- Linear
- Vercel
- Notion

Characteristics:

- Clean spacing
- Soft shadows
- Rounded cards
- Calm animations
- Minimal clutter

---

# Writing Style

Short paragraphs.

Clear headings.

Simple language.

Avoid long sentences.

Always explain benefits before features.

---

# Email Style

Professional.

Personal.

Respectful.

No spam language.

Always include:

- Name
- Company
- Contact information
- Clear CTA

---

# Sales Presentation Style

Focus on:

- Business problems
- Live demonstration
- ROI
- Simplicity

Avoid feature overload.

# Brand Checklist

Every new asset should answer:

- Does it look premium?
- Is it easy to understand?
- Does it build trust?
- Does it reflect healthcare professionalism?
- Does it reinforce PatientPilot AI's mission?

If not, revise it.
