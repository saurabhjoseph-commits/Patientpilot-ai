# Lead Qualification

Ask:

- How many patient calls do you receive each month?
- How many front desk staff members answer phones?
- What happens after hours?
- How are appointments scheduled?
- What software do you currently use?
- What challenges are your staff facing?

# Common Objections

## "We're too small."

Response

Many smaller practices benefit the most because every missed call has a greater impact on revenue.

---

## "We already have a receptionist."

Response

PatientPilot AI supports your receptionist by answering after-hours calls, reducing repetitive work, and ensuring every patient receives a response.

---

## "AI won't sound human."

Response

Let's listen to a real conversation together. Our goal is a natural, professional patient experience.

---

## "It's too expensive."

Response

Compare the cost of the platform to the revenue from even one or two recovered appointments each month.

---

## "We need to think about it."

Response

Absolutely. I'll send a summary and answer any additional questions. Would it be helpful to schedule a brief follow-up next week?

---

# Pricing Discussion

Focus on value before discussing price.

Avoid leading with cost.

Discuss:

- Missed revenue
- Staff efficiency
- Patient experience
- Appointment recovery

Then present pricing.
