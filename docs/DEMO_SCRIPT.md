# Purpose

This document defines the official PatientPilot AI customer demonstration.

Every live demo should follow this structure.

Goals:

- Build trust
- Demonstrate value
- Personalize the experience
- Handle objections
- Secure the next step

Target Duration

15 Minutes

---

# Demo Objective

At the end of the presentation the prospect should believe:

✓ The AI sounds professional.

✓ The system understands dental practices.

✓ This will reduce staff workload.

✓ This will help capture more patients.

✓ The investment makes business sense.

---

## Technical Checklist

✓ AI conversation tested

# Scene 1

## Welcome

Duration

2 Minutes

---

Say:

> Thank you for taking the time to meet today.

> I spent some time learning about your practice before our call because I wanted to make this demonstration relevant to your team.

---

Transition

Today I want to show you how PatientPilot AI helps dental practices answer every patient call, reduce front desk workload, and book more appointments.

---

# Scene 2

## Learn About the Practice

Ask questions.

Examples:

How many calls does your office receive on a typical day?

How many team members answer the phones?

What happens when someone calls after hours?

Do patients ever reach voicemail?

What are the biggest challenges for your front desk team?

Listen carefully.

Take notes.

Do not interrupt.

---

# Scene 3

## Introduce the Problem

Summarize.

Example

"So it sounds like your biggest challenges are after-hours calls, missed opportunities, and keeping the front desk focused during busy periods."

Pause.

Allow the prospect to agree.

---

# Scene 4

## Demo Center

Open

```
Demo Center
```

Explain

"This is where we prepare a personalized demonstration for each practice."

Show:

- Practice profile
- Logo
- Dentist name
- Services
- Branding

Keep this section under one minute.

---

# Scene 5

## Incoming Patient Call

Launch Presentation.

Phone rings.

Allow one second before AI answers.

---

AI says

> Thank you for calling Bright Smile Dental.

> This is Emma, your virtual receptionist.

> How may I help you today?

Remain silent.

Let the prospect listen.

---

# Scene 6

## AI Conversation

Run the complete conversation.

Scenario

New patient requesting a cleaning appointment.

Conversation should demonstrate:

- Natural greeting
- Patient name capture
- Phone number capture
- Appointment request
- Insurance question
- Available times
- Confirmation
- Friendly closing

Do not interrupt the conversation.

---

# Scene 7

## Live CRM

Immediately after booking.

Show

Patient appears.

Lead created.

Appointment scheduled.

Transcript saved.

Everything updates automatically.

Say

"Notice that nobody had to type any of this."

Pause.

Allow them to absorb the value.

---

# Scene 8

## Analytics

Navigate to Analytics.

Explain

Every interaction becomes measurable.

Show

- Calls answered
- Appointments booked
- AI conversations
- Revenue opportunities

Do not spend more than two minutes here.

---

# Scene 9

## Business Value

Focus on outcomes.

Examples

Instead of missing calls...

Now every caller receives a professional response.

Instead of manual data entry...

Everything is captured automatically.

Instead of guessing...

You'll have measurable insights.

---

# Scene 10

## Pricing

Transition

"Now that you've seen how it works, let's talk about how practices typically get started."

Explain pricing.

Avoid technical discussion.

Focus on ROI.

---

# Scene 11

## Questions

Invite questions.

Never rush.

Answer honestly.

If unsure,

Say

"I'll confirm that after the meeting."

Never guess.

---

# Scene 12

## Close

Recommended close

"I'd love to work with your team on a pilot implementation.

The next step would be a short onboarding session where we personalize PatientPilot AI for your practice."

Ask

"Would you be comfortable scheduling that?"

Remain silent.

Let them answer.

---

# Demo Rules

Never rush.

Never criticize competitors.

Never exaggerate.

Never overpromise.

Always focus on solving business problems.

---

# Common Objections

## We already have a receptionist.

Response

PatientPilot AI complements your team by handling repetitive calls, after-hours inquiries, and peak call volume.

---

## AI sounds robotic.

Response

Let's listen to another real conversation together.

---

## We need to think about it.

Response

That's completely understandable. I'll send a summary of today's discussion and answer any additional questions you may have.

---

## It's too expensive.

Response

Consider the value of recovering just a few additional appointments each month compared to the cost of the platform.

---

# Success Criteria

A successful demo means:

- Prospect understood the value
- Questions were answered
- Next step was agreed
- Follow-up scheduled

Closing immediately is a bonus, not the only measure of success.

---

# Presenter Tips

Speak slowly.

Smile.

Pause after important points.

Use the prospect's practice name throughout the conversation.

Let the AI do the talking during the demo.

Do not narrate over the AI.

---

# Golden Rule

The purpose of the demo is not to impress the prospect with technology.

The purpose is to help them clearly imagine how PatientPilot AI can improve their practice, support their staff, and create a better experience for every patient.
